All that you need to know about Tech Stack

Important Dates:

  • Registration Deadline: 5th April 2025
  • Submission Deadline: 8th April 2025 (11:59 PM IST)
  • The theme will be given after the registration closes.

Submission Requirements:

Each team must submit the following:

  • Presentation (PPT/PDF) Explain your idea, problem statement, solution, and features.
  • GitHub Repository Share the source code with proper documentation.
  • Live Hosted Link Provide a working demo of the app/web app.
  • Bonus: Teams can also submit a short video demo (optional) explaining their project.
  • Participants can use any technology or framework to build their project.
  • The project must be an app (mobile or web-based solution).

Judging Criteria:

Projects will be evaluated based on:

  • Concept & Innovation (30%) How unique and impactful is the idea?
  • Execution & Functionality (30%) How well is the app implemented?
  • User Experience & Design (20%) Is the UI intuitive and engaging?
  • Presentation & Documentation (20%) Clarity and completeness of the PPT and GitHub README.

Rules & Guidelines:

  • Team Size: 2-3 members in each team.
  • Originality: The project must be built specifically for this hackathon. Plagiarism or reusing old projects will lead to disqualification.
  • Open Source: The GitHub repository must be public for evaluation.
  • Respect & Ethics: No hate speech, discrimination, or harmful content is allowed.
  • Working Prototype: Your submission should be a functioning prototype, not just an idea.
  • Participants can use open-source tools.
  • Identification of plagiarism in the code will lead to disqualification.

How to Submit?

  • Submit your PPT, GitHub Repo link, and live-hosted app link via the Submission Portal/Form link, which will be provided after the registration deadline.
  • Deadline: 8th April 2025, 11:59 PM IST

Prizes & Recognition:

  • Winner: Best concept & execution wins.
  • Runner-ups: Special mentions for unique ideas.
  • Impact Award: Best project with strong real-world impact.

Important dates & deadlines?

  • Registration Deadline 05 Apr 25, 11:59 AM IST
