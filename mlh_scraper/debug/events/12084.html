Event Description

Howdy, Friends!

From email providers to spacecrafts -- everything around us is being made smarter with the use of Artificial Intelligence. This weekend, build hacks that can think and take decisions even better than humans!

First time hacking with AI? Get started by watching tutorials from Global Hack Week: AI/ML.

Whether you build something on the theme or not, focus on having fun. While we'd love to have you build something on the theme, our themes are only here for inspiration. Please build whatever you'd like!

Happy Hacking!
